Web Design & Development

Avha Creations E-Commerce Platform

A custom e-commerce solution that transformed a local business into a digital success story

CLIENT

Avha Creations

TIMELINE

8 Weeks

ROLE

UI/UX Design, Development

YEAR

2024

Project Overview

Avha Creations, a local artisanal crafts business, approached me with a challenge: transform their offline-only business into an online powerhouse that could showcase their unique handcrafted products while creating a seamless shopping experience for their customers.

The goal was to create a fully responsive e-commerce platform that highlighted the quality and craftsmanship of their products.

The fully responsive homepage design showcases featured products and brand story

My Approach

I approached this project with a comprehensive strategy that focused on both aesthetic appeal and functional excellence. My process involved:

01

Discovery & Research

I began with an in-depth discovery session to understand Avha's brand identity, target audience, and business goals. This was followed by market research to identify e-commerce best practices specific to artisanal products and to analyze competitor websites.

02

User Experience Design

After establishing the project requirements, I created user personas. This informed the wireframing and information architecture, ensuring an intuitive and frictionless user experience.

03

Visual Design

The UI design phase focused on creating a visual language that reflected the handcrafted nature of Avha's products while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic. I developed a custom color palette and typography system that enhanced the brand identity.

04

Development & Integration

I built the website using modern web technologies, ensuring it was fully responsive and optimized for performance. Special attention was paid to the product catalog.

05

Testing & Launch

Rigorous testing across devices and browsers ensured a flawless user experience. After addressing all issues, we launched the site.

Design System

A cohesive design system was created to ensure consistency across the platform while reflecting Avha Creations' artisanal brand identity.

Technology Stack

HTML CSS JavaScript

Key Features

The final platform includes several features designed to enhance the user experience and boost conversions:

  • Immersive Product Galleries - High-quality image displays with zoom functionality to showcase product details
  • Mobile-First Design - Ensuring a seamless experience across all devices
